Why Development?

After studying Industrial design and applying my knowledge of planning, prototyping, researching and developing an idea to a tanigle product I looked at other ways that I could do the same but in a different way. I tried out a number of online courses and after comming accross General Assembly. I did a short course and then a 12-week intensive bootcamp in Web Development. After that I was hooked, solving problems in a digital way.
